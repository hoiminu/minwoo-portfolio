About Me

The non-resume version.

Hi there!

I'm a product manager with hands-on software engineering experience, based in Seattle and currently at Meta working on monetization and platform products that reach billions of users. Before Meta, I spent nearly a decade at Amazon, shipping GenAI-powered Alexa features, scaling global supply chains, launching grocery delivery across three countries, and building a luxury watches category from scratch.

My career started in luxury retail in Seoul at Shilla Duty Free (Samsung Group), where I managed a $111M portfolio of watch brands. I went on to earn my MBA at Columbia Business School, interned at Louis Vuitton in New York, and eventually made my way to tech. I also completed Amazon's Technical Academy for software engineering and General Assembly's UX Design program.

I speak three languages: Korean (native), Japanese (JLPT N1), and English. I love bridging worlds, whether that's across cultures, between engineering and business, or from customer pain points to scalable solutions.

When I'm not heads-down on a product problem, you'll find me on the golf course, at a pottery studio, curled up with a book, or hanging out with my dogs.
